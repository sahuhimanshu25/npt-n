### 2. Location Privacy (Foursquare Study)

> **Foursquare** is a Location-Based Social Network (LBSN).

#### Key Features

```
Check-ins   →  User marks presence at a venue
Tips        →  Reviews/comments left at locations
Mayorship   →  Awarded to person with most check-ins at a venue in last 60 days (gamification)
```

#### Key Findings

- ✅ Human mobility is **highly regular and predictable**
- ✅ **Home location** can be inferred with high accuracy (within a few km) using check-in patterns and mayorship data
- ✅ Even without a direct home address, repeated patterns reveal sensitive location information

> **Broader Implication:** Self-disclosure (check-ins, location-tagged photos) + advanced inference techniques seriously threaten location privacy.

### 3. Policing on Online Social Media

#### Historical Milestone

> 📅 **2009 – US Airways Flight 1549 (Hudson River landing)** First major real-world crisis where citizens used **Twitter** to report live _before_ official responders arrived. Marked the shift of social media from personal chatter → **crisis communication tool**.

#### Police Strategies

- Community engagement via hashtags: `#myNYPD`, `#myLAPD`
    - ⚠️ Often **backfired** – users posted negative/critical content instead of appreciation
- Traffic updates, crime alerts, appreciation posts
- Rewards for information, public announcements

#### Indian Context

|Police Force|Platform|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|Bangalore City Police (CPBLR)|Twitter + Facebook|Very active & popular|
|Delhi Traffic Police|Twitter + Facebook|High engagement|
|Hyderabad City Police|Twitter + Facebook|Active adopter|

- Most pages **allow public posting/comments**
- **Verified Accounts (Blue Tick):** Critical for authenticity; only a small fraction of accounts are verified

#### Major Challenge – Masquerading / Fake Accounts

```
Problem  →  Many fake handles impersonating police departments
Impact   →  Citizens cannot distinguish real vs. fake accounts
Solution →  Centralized tracking page listing legitimate handles
             with verification status + official government website links
```
